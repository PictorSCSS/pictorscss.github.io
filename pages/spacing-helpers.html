Spacing Helpers

Overview

  • Positions are abbreviated by 1 or 2 letters, followed by the size

    • -l-: Left
    • -r-: Right
    • -t-: Top
    • -b-: Bottom
    • -tb-: Top and Bottom
    • -lr-: Left and Right
  • So, for example, you can do declare sm padding only on the top of the element by adding the class: padding-t-sm
