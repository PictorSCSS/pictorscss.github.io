Grids

Overview

  • Pictor grids are traditional 12 column grids, but based on display: inline-block; instead of floats and margins.
  • Grid containers .grid or .grid-fw set the font size to 0.
  • Child .column or .item elements reset font-size to $font-size
  • Immediately after .grid or .grid-fw element, child element must contain the class .column or .item, or they will not display its contents.
  • Columns are percentage based, so the grids adjust to the width of its container, or whatever you set it to.
